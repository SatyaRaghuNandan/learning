Situational Questions:
1. Tell me about a difficult co-worker who you're having a conflict with.
2. Accenture: Tell me about a time when you had to make a split second decision.
3. Tell me about a time when you had a problem working in a group.
4. Describe a time when you convinced someone who initially disagreed with you to follow your recommendation. 
5. Tell me how you saved your company money.
6. Tell me about an experience where you had to take on a leadership role.
7. Tell me about a time when you had to use logical thinking, an what was the outcome.
8. Tell me about an occasion when you had to resolve a complex problem in a team.
