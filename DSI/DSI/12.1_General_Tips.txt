====== 12.1 General Tips ======
Created Friday 17 February 2017

Data Science/ML is mostly about Pattern Detection, find hidden patterns that people can't easily
find by EDA, otherwise it's just Data Analysis

Two principles in Computer Science:
	1. Don't Repeat Yourself
	2. Keep It Simple
	Best way to memorize is to practice
	Best way to practice is to make the practice fun
